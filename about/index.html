About us

Arcadia Carts and Arizona Mobile Golf Car Service are divisions of Arizona Mobile Services, LLC

20+ Years of Golf Cart Excellence

Arizona Mobile Golf Car Service (AZMGCS) is a family owned and operated golf car business, founded by Mario Violi in 2000.

As a child, Mario spent time working with his father on farm equipment and automobiles. During this time Mario learned every aspect of mechanics, and how the right care and knowledge could extend the life and performance of any vehicle. Mario could not help but have a warm feeling every time his father would deliver a repaired vehicle to someone who could not afford a new one. When Mario relocated to Arizona, he partnered with his father-in-law, Ernie Taraborelli, previous owner-partner of D&T Golf Cars. Mario and Ernie created V&T Battery Doctors/Golf Cars. Even though Mario was learning the most advanced techniques in the golf car/electric vehicle industry, he never forgot the lessons he learned from his father or the gratitude of those his father helped all those years ago. Mario was determined to honor his father by opening a business that would truly offer value to his customers. He promised himself to always provide value, quality and outstanding service, regardless of how large his business grew.

Mario opened Arizona Mobile Golf Car Service in 2000. Since then he has been providing quality service and offering outstanding value to customers throughout the Valley of the Sun.
